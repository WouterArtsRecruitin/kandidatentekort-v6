## üìû TROUBLESHOOTING

**If CTR < 1% in Week 1:**
- Pause Authority ad (1.4) - cold audience not ready yet
- Boost Curiosity (1.3) - higher engagement expected
- Check copy matches design text

**If CPL > ‚Ç¨20 in Week 2:**
- Landing page friction check (form length?)
- Ad copy may be mismatched
- Try social proof + numbers combo (2.2)

**If no conversions in Week 3:**
- Check form is working on landing page
- Verify conversion pixel is firing
- Re-run Week 1-2 ads (warmer message)

**If retargeting doesn't work:**
- Audience size may be small (need >10k pixels)
- Try broader lookalike (1%)
- Reduce frequency cap (max 2 ads/day)
